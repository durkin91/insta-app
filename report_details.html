About Our Process

When generating your report, we looked at all 350,000 of @taleishabonoraa's followers.

For each follower, we assessed a variety of criteria such as their number of posts, number of followers, number of following, recorded locations, engagement on their posts, and privacy settings on their account.

We then used this data to categorize followers as real or fake and/or unengaged.

Most accounts naturally posess at least a small amount of fake followers who were not paid for. If an account carried an unusually large percentage of fake followers, they were most likely paid for.
